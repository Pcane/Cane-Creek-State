Mixing Directions
1
Night before: Sprinkle dry bentonite into warm water. Do not stir -- let slake overnight.
2
Weigh all dry materials except bentonite. Set aside.
3
Pre-mix barium carbonate -- N100 mask required. First mix barium carbonate with 500g batch material. Then 3-4 lb. Then full batch.
4
Combine all dry materials and mix thoroughly.
5
Add bentonite slurry to dry materials and mix.
6
Add remaining water gradually: total. Mix and check consistency -- feel is the guide.
7
Pug immediately in Peter Pugger with vacuum deairing.
!
Barium Carbonate Warning: Toxic by inhalation. N100 respirator required for all dry mixing.
